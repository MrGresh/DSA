# Dynamic Programming
Optimization method involving breaking down problems into simpler subproblems and storing their solutions.

---

## 1D / Linear DP
Track optimal solution using a 1D array → sequences, sums, or counts.

| # | Problem | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Climbing Stairs | 🟢 Easy |
| 2 | House Robber | 🟡 Medium |
| 3 | Maximum Subarray | 🟡 Medium |
| 4 | Maximum Product Subarray | 🟡 Medium |
| 5 | Decode Ways | 🟡 Medium |
